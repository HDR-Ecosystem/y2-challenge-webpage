Scientific Modeling out of distribution (Scientific-Mood) ML Challenge


The HDR ML Challenge program is hosting its second FAIR challenge, this year presenting three scientific benchmarks for modeling out of distribution in three critical areas: Neural Forecasting, Climate Prediction using Ecological Data, and Coastal Flooding Prediction over time.
