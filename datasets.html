Datasets

Training datasets

These are labeled datasets that allow the proponents to train their algorithms. The goal here is to provide some comfort with the dataset format and how to process the information. The datasets include targets to enable the training. However, since the goal of this challenge is to extrapoloate, the full parameter space (time and/or channels) in the final dataset are incomplete. The goal of this dataset is to understand the behavior.

Validation/evaluation datasets

We provide a private validation dataset to allow participants to test their models for submission. The evaluation metrics match those of the overall test dataset scoring. This evaluation (and the inference) is performed on the Codabench server, which currently consists of A10s on the San Diego Super Computing cluster's NSF National Research Platform.

Final Challenge datasets

Performance on the Final challenge datasets will determine challenge scores. Please see the individual challenges for more details on their evaluation. Submission scoring code is provided on each challenge's GitHub repository as well. These datasets will be publicly released after the competition. As with validation, the scoring is performed on the Codabench server, which currently consists of A10s on the San Diego Super Computing cluster's NSF National Research Platform.
